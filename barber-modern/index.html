Our Heritage

Since 1925, Sharps Barber Co. has been the cornerstone of men's grooming in our community. We preserve the timeless traditions of barbering while embracing the craftsmanship that made this profession an art form.

Our vintage-inspired shop features original fixtures, classic styling chairs, and the authentic atmosphere that transports you to a simpler time when a haircut was more than just a service—it was an experience.

Our Work

Our Master Barbers

William "Bill" Thompson

Master Barber & Shop Owner

With over 25 years of experience, Bill learned the craft from his grandfather who opened this shop in 1925. He specializes in traditional cuts and straight razor shaves, keeping the vintage techniques alive for a new generation.

Frank Rodriguez

Senior Barber

Frank brings 15 years of experience and a passion for classic styling. He's known for his attention to detail and ability to create the perfect vintage look for any gentleman.

James "Jimmy" Wilson

Classic Style Specialist

Jimmy specializes in vintage and retro styles, bringing back the classic looks from the 1920s through the 1960s. His expertise in period-accurate cuts makes him a favorite among vintage enthusiasts.
