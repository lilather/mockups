About Great Smiles Dentistry

Dedicated to creating beautiful, healthy smiles

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Great Smiles Dentistry began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional dental care in a warm, welcoming environment where patients feel like family. What started as a small practice has grown into one of the most trusted dental offices in the community.

Over the past 15 years, we've helped thousands of patients achieve healthy, beautiful smiles through comprehensive dental care, advanced technology, and compassionate treatment. Our commitment to excellence and patient satisfaction has remained unwavering throughout our growth.

Today, our team of experienced dental professionals continues to uphold the founding principles of our practice while embracing the latest advances in dental technology and treatment methods.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide exceptional dental care with compassion, technology, and expertise, creating beautiful, healthy smiles while ensuring every patient feels comfortable, valued, and well-cared for throughout their dental journey.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected dental practice in our community, known for our commitment to excellence, patient advocacy, and positive impact on oral health and overall well-being.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We treat every patient with kindness, empathy, and understanding, recognizing that dental visits can be stressful.

Patient Focus

Our patients' needs, comfort, and satisfaction are our top priority in every decision we make.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from clinical care to customer service.

Integrity

We conduct ourselves with honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our interactions.
