Our Bar Selections

Curated whiskeys, bourbons, and signature cocktails for true connoisseurs.

Whiskeys & Bourbons

  • Bulleit Bourbon
    A high‑rye bourbon with bold spice, smooth vanilla, and toasted oak notes.
  • Maker’s Mark
    Soft, wheated bourbon offering caramel, toffee, and gentle spice on the finish.
  • Woodford Reserve
    Rich and complex—dried fruit, cinnamon, dark chocolate, and toasted oak blend.
  • Jack Daniel’s
    Tennessee whiskey charcoal‑filtered for banana, toasted oak, and warm spice flavors.
  • Jameson Irish Whiskey
    Triple‑distilled, light and floral with honeyed sweetness and soft vanilla undertones.

Signature Cocktails

  • Old Fashioned
    Bourbon stirred with bitters and sugar, garnished with a fresh orange twist.
  • Manhattan
    Rye whiskey blended with sweet vermouth and bitters, served up with a cherry.
  • Whiskey Sour
    Bourbon shaken with lemon juice and syrup for a bright, balanced sip.
  • Mint Julep
    Bourbon over crushed ice with mint leaves and simple syrup—refreshingly cool.
  • Barbershop Boulevardier
    Bourbon, Campari, and sweet vermouth unite in a bold, bittersweet classic.
