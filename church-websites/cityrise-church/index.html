Cityrise Church started with a handful of people who believed faith should be lived out loud. Today we are one church gathering across three campuses, united by a simple conviction: courage grows when people are connected to Christ and to each other.

We are ordinary people chasing an extraordinary God. Come as you are, bring your questions, and discover a community that has room for you.
