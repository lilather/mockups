Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the service?

Our services typically last about 60 minutes.

What should I wear?

Whatever you're comfortable in. Most people dress casually.

Is there childcare available?

Yes, we provide safe and fun programs for children of all ages.

Will I be asked to give money?

No. Giving is for members only. Guests are never expected to give.
