Welcome Home

A Holy Spirit-Led Church in Maple Grove

We're a warm, growing family of believers in Maple Grove, Illinois — gathering each Sunday to encounter God, grow in His Word, and love our neighbors well. Wherever you are on your journey, there's a seat for you.

In Person

Sunday Gatherings

Every Sunday we gather for heartfelt worship, prayer, and teaching straight from the Scriptures. Services are at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM at our Maple Grove campus. Come early for coffee — our Welcome Team would love to meet you.
